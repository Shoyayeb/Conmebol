All highlights Copa America 2021

The 2021 Copa América Final was the 47th final of the Copa América, The match was held at Maracanã Stadium in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil on 10 July 2021. The match featured two rival teams: Argentina and the tournament hosts, Brazil. Argentina won the match 1–0 to clinch their fifteenth Copa América title and first since 1993, giving captain Lionel Messi his first senior international trophy sixteen years into his career.
